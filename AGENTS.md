- --help text (help.txt) and "ergo quickstart" output (quickstart.txt) is incredibly important because these sources are the complete manual for agents (our primary users). They seek to be:
    - succinct, streamlined, fully accurate, and not redundant
    - 100% coverage of tool surface area
    - a great example of information design, for humans
